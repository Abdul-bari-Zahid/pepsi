About Pepsi

PepsiCo, Inc. is one of the world’s largest food and beverage companies, serving over 200 countries with a wide range of delicious products. Founded in 1898 by Caleb Bradham, Pepsi has grown into a global icon, known for its refreshing taste, innovative marketing, and commitment to sustainability.

Our Mission

At Pepsi, our mission is to create more smiles with every sip and every bite. We aim to deliver moments of joy through our products, while also making a positive impact on the planet and society. We believe in sustainability, diversity, and community involvement as core pillars of our business.

Our History

What started as a small soda shop in North Carolina has transformed into a global empire. Over the years, Pepsi has evolved from its original recipe to include a wide variety of beverages and snacks. Our legacy is built on innovation, from introducing diet sodas to leading campaigns for social change.

Sustainability

We are committed to sustainable growth. Our PepsiCo Positive strategy aims to inspire change by sourcing ingredients responsibly, reducing water use, cutting greenhouse gas emissions, and supporting sustainable farming practices. Our goal is to build a healthier future for people and the planet.

Innovation

Pepsi has always been at the forefront of innovation. From our groundbreaking marketing campaigns to developing healthier product alternatives, we consistently aim to stay ahead in the ever-changing beverage industry. Our research and development teams are constantly working on new flavors, packaging designs, and eco-friendly initiatives.

Commitment to Diversity

We believe in creating a workplace where everyone feels valued and included. Our team is made up of diverse individuals from all backgrounds, and we are committed to building an inclusive environment where innovation thrives. Pepsi’s commitment to diversity extends beyond our company, as we support community initiatives and global programs that promote equality and inclusion.
